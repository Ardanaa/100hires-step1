# Expert Newsletters & Email Content

A key source of expert insights comes from newsletters. These practitioner-focused newsletters contain original analysis, experiments, and frameworks that are often more detailed than social media posts.

---

## Active Newsletters (2026)

### 1. Growth Memo — Kevin Indig
- **URL:** https://www.growth-memo.com/
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Focus:** Data-driven SEO experiments, growth loops, AI impact on organic traffic
- **Why it matters:** Kevin publishes original research and data analysis weekly — not rehashed opinions. His coverage of AI's impact on organic growth includes traffic studies across multiple SaaS verticals.
- **Key recent topics (2026):**
  - Impact of AI Overviews on click-through rates by industry
  - Growth loops that survive AI search disruption
  - How SaaS companies are adapting SEO strategy post-AI Overviews

---

### 2. SEOFOMO — Aleyda Solis
- **URL:** https://www.seofomo.co/
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Focus:** Curated SEO news, AI search updates, tool recommendations
- **Why it matters:** The most comprehensive weekly roundup of SEO developments, with Aleyda's expert curation filtering signal from noise. Regularly covers AI crawler behavior differences and GEO/AEO developments.
- **Key recent topics (2026):**
  - AI crawler optimization techniques
  - GEO/AEO strategy updates
  - International SEO in the age of AI translation

---

### 3. Product-Led SEO Newsletter — Eli Schwartz
- **URL:** https://www.productledseo.com/
- **Subtitle:** "The Future of SEO & AEO"
- **Frequency:** Biweekly
- **Focus:** SEO as a product function, AI-era content strategy
- **Why it matters:** Eli's framework reframes content production away from volume toward product utility — critical for B2B SaaS companies where AI content mills produce diminishing returns.
- **Key recent topics (2026):**
  - Why chasing AI citations is a losing strategy
  - Product-Led SEO in the AI era
  - Google AI Mode implications for content strategy

---

### 4. SEO Notebook — Steve Toth
- **URL:** https://seonotebook.com/
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Focus:** Unconventional, high-leverage SEO tactics
- **Why it matters:** One of the most subscribed practitioner-focused SEO newsletters. Steve writes from in-house SaaS experience (FreshBooks) and documents what actually works vs. what sounds good in theory.
- **Key recent topics (2026):**
  - User Embedding edge in AI search
  - Query Fan-Out optimization
  - Brand truth framework for AI search
