## SEO & Content Optimization Tools

### Clearscope — Bernard Huang
- **URL:** https://www.clearscope.io/
- **Type:** AI-powered content optimization platform
- **Used by:** HubSpot, Adobe, Condé Nast, Shopify
- **Relevance:** The industry standard for AI-assisted content grading. Clearscope uses NLP to analyze top-ranking content and provide optimization recommendations. Bernard's insights come from seeing how thousands of enterprise teams use AI for content at scale.

### Rankability — Nathan Gotch
- **URL:** https://www.rankability.com/
- **Type:** SEO + AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) software for agencies
- **Relevance:** One of the first tools to combine traditional SEO and AEO workflows into a single platform. Nathan's tutorials directly document how teams use Rankability for AI-optimized content production.

### PageOptimizer Pro (POP) — Kyle Roof
- **URL:** https://pageoptimizer.pro/
- **Type:** Scientific on-page SEO optimization tool
- **US Patent:** Kyle holds a US patent for his SEO testing methodology
- **Relevance:** POP uses data from 400+ peer-reviewed SEO tests. Kyle's approach to AI content optimization is rooted in empirical testing, not conjecture — exactly what AI SEO production workflows need.
