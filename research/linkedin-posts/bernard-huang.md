**Topic:** From backlinks to brand mentions — the AEO shift  

> In an LLM-driven world, brand mentions and co-mentions are becoming increasingly important compared to traditional backlinks.
>
> When AI models synthesize answers, they rely on patterns of brand association across the web. If authoritative sources consistently mention your brand alongside a topic, the AI develops "trust" in your relevance.
>
> This doesn't mean backlinks are dead. But the mechanism is different. It's less about link equity and more about semantic association.
>
> The validation layer is critical: ensure your content is factually robust and structured in a way that AI models can easily ground their answers in your material.
>
> We're seeing this play out across Clearscope customers at HubSpot, Adobe, and Condé Nast — the ones investing in brand mentions outperform those still focused purely on link building.

**Key Takeaway:** AEO shifts the emphasis from backlinks to brand mentions and semantic associations. AI models build "trust" through patterns of brand co-occurrence across authoritative sources, not just link equity.
